About the Conference

Ever since its inception, the ICRTMDR conference has sought to serve as a beacon of forethought and consciousness on everything that has to do with the twin worlds of engineering and technology. No matter how prestigious the university or academic institution one may be enrolled in or affiliated with, a high-level virtual conference such as ICRTMDR is without a shadow of a doubt, the best way for anyone (be they a student, an educator, scientist, or industry professional) involved in these fields, to get up-to-speed with the -

  • Freshest insights from groundbreaking research studies.
  • Burgeoning technological developments being made worldwide.
  • Brand new engineering tools and techniques
  • Know-how on how to write and publish pioneering research papers especially suited for modern audiences, and plenty more
